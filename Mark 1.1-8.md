Mark 1:1-8



## Verse Text

> [!NRSV]
> 
> 1 The beginning of the good news[[a](https://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=Mark%201%3A1-8&version=NRSVUE#fen-NRSVUE-24214a "See footnote a")] of Jesus Christ.[[b](https://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=Mark%201%3A1-8&version=NRSVUE#fen-NRSVUE-24214b "See footnote b")]
> 
> 2 As it is written in the prophet Isaiah,[[c](https://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=Mark%201%3A1-8&version=NRSVUE#fen-NRSVUE-24215c "See footnote c")]
> 
> “See, I am sending my messenger ahead of you,[[d](https://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=Mark%201%3A1-8&version=NRSVUE#fen-NRSVUE-24215d "See footnote d")]  
>     who will prepare your way,  
> 3 the voice of one crying out in the wilderness:  
>     ‘Prepare the way of the Lord;  
>     make his paths straight,’ ”
> 
> 4 so John the baptizer appeared[[e](https://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=Mark%201%3A1-8&version=NRSVUE#fen-NRSVUE-24217e "See footnote e")] in the wilderness, proclaiming a baptism of repentance for the forgiveness of sins. 5 And the whole Judean region and all the people of Jerusalem were going out to him and were baptized by him in the River Jordan, confessing their sins. 6 Now John was clothed with camel’s hair, with a leather belt around his waist, and he ate locusts and wild honey. 7 He proclaimed, “The one who is more powerful than I is coming after me; I am not worthy to stoop down and untie the strap of his sandals. 8 I have baptized you with[[f](https://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=Mark%201%3A1-8&version=NRSVUE#fen-NRSVUE-24221f "See footnote f")] water, but he will baptize you with[[g](https://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=Mark%201%3A1-8&version=NRSVUE#fen-NRSVUE-24221g "See footnote g")] the Holy Spirit.”
